The Platform

Five pillars.
One direction.

Future Forward is not a collection of ideas — it is a structured roadmap grounded in chapter insights and lived experience, designed to elevate GIL's operations, impact, and brotherhood for the long term.

01

Committee Excellence & Shared Ownership

A structured, accountable, and collaborative system for committee work — where every brother contributes meaningfully to chapter execution.

  • Clear roles and responsibilities across all committees
  • Monthly progress checkpoints and post-event recaps
  • Special Events Committee for MLK Day of Service & Juneteenth Gala
  • Cross-committee collaboration to align service, social & education efforts
02

$75K Fundraising Commitment & Revenue Strategy

A structured model to grow GIL's financial capacity through grants, corporate partnerships, and experiential fundraising — 7.5× growth from 2026 to 2027.

  • MLK Day of Service Sponsorship: $5,000
  • Social Event Series (2 events): $20,000
  • Grants from Wells Fargo, BofA, Citi & Rockefeller Foundations
  • Year-over-year growth of 30–40% with dedicated Fundraising Committee
03

Juneteenth Go-To-College Gala

A reimagined signature tradition and GIL's premier annual fundraising and impact event — hosted at the Weeksville Heritage Center, connecting cultural legacy with educational investment.

  • Formal scholarship recognition for college-bound students
  • Community and partner reception with alumni storytelling
  • Direct link between fundraising and $2K–$4K student scholarships
  • Modernized continuation of a historic chapter tradition

04

Leadership Continuity & Next Gen Development

Sustainable chapters are built when leadership is continuously developed, not repeatedly reset. Building the next generation with intention.

  • Structured leadership transition systems across roles
  • Mentorship and readiness pathways for new brothers
  • 50% increase in reclaimed brother engagement year over year
  • Leadership development embedded into committee structure
05

Brand Elevation & Chapter Visibility

Our chapter's impact is only as strong as its ability to be understood, recognized, and respected. Elevating how GIL is seen internally and externally.

  • Standardized communication and presentation systems
  • 5–8 external partnerships annually
  • Elevated professionalism across all chapter outputs
  • A unified identity rooted in excellence, unity, and pride

About the Candidate

Built for this
moment.

"As we climb, we have a responsibility to lift those coming behind us."

Tommy Atkins is a results-driven professional with more than a decade of experience driving over $100 million in revenue growth and brand innovation. His career has spanned Condé Nast, Business Insider, Hearst Magazines, and Vogue — building complex partnerships, managing multimillion-dollar budgets, and executing high-impact campaigns recognized by The Clio Awards, Ad Age, CFDA, and Digiday.

Born and raised in Saginaw, Michigan and a proud graduate of Howard University, Tommy brings the perspective of someone who has navigated systems, built pipelines of access, and never lost sight of where he came from.

At the core of his work — and his candidacy — is the belief that the strongest partnerships are built on trust, insights, and creativity. That belief extends directly to how he envisions leading Gamma Iota Lambda: a chapter defined by shared ownership, elevated execution, and a brotherhood that uplifts every member.
